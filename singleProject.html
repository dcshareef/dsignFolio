    Description

    As part of my internship at Haris and Co, I took on the challenge of creating a dummy agency landing page to explore the dynamics of web design. This project was a hands-on exercise in combining modern aesthetics with user-friendly functionality. The goal was to craft a layout that not only looked professional but also offered intuitive navigation and clear communication of the agency's core message. Every element, from the hero section to the contact form, was designed to reflect a balance of simplicity and sophistication.

    Through this project, I honed my skills in layout design, typography, and visual hierarchy. The clean color palette, bold typography, and well-structured sections ensure the design is both engaging and effective. This exercise allowed me to experiment with key principles of branding and user interface design, making it a valuable addition to my growing portfolio..
